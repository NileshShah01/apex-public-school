Academic Programs

Apex Public School provides academic programs from Play Group to Class 8. The curriculum focuses on strong foundational knowledge and prepares students for future competitive examinations.

Pre Primary Section

Play Group, Nursery, LKG, UKG

Early childhood learning through play activities, motor skill development and social interaction. The focus remains on building curiosity and interest in learning.

Primary Section

Class 1 to Class 5

Students build strong foundations in English, Hindi, Mathematics, Science and Social Studies while developing reading, writing and problem solving skills.

Middle School

Class 6 to Class 8

Students develop analytical thinking and deeper subject knowledge to prepare for higher education and competitive examinations.
