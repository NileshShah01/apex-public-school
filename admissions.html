Admission Open

Admissions are open for the academic session 2026 to 2027. Apex Public School is enrolling students from Play Group to Class 8.

Admission Process

  1. Visit the school office or contact the school for admission enquiry.
  2. Collect and fill the admission application form.
  3. Submit the required documents.
  4. Pay the admission fee to confirm admission.

Required Documents

  • Birth Certificate
  • Transfer Certificate (if student studied in another school)
  • Previous class mark sheet
  • Aadhar Card copy of student and parents
  • Passport size photographs
  • Address proof

Age Criteria

  • Play Group: 2 plus years
  • Nursery: 3 plus years
  • LKG: 4 plus years
  • UKG: 5 plus years
  • Class 1: 6 plus years

Age is considered as on 31 March of the admission year.

Fee Structure

The school offers affordable education so that quality learning remains accessible to every child. For complete fee details, please contact the school office.

Why Choose Apex Public School

  • Experienced and caring teachers
  • Safe and disciplined environment
  • Modern classrooms and facilities
  • Focus on academic and moral development
  • Affordable education
  • Individual attention to students
