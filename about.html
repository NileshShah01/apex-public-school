About Apex Public School

Welcome to Apex Public School

Apex Public School located in Anjani Bazar, Parsa, Saran provides quality education from Play Group to Class 8. The school focuses on strong academic foundation, discipline and character development.

Our institution supports students from every background and prepares them for future competitive examinations and responsible citizenship.

Principal Message

Education builds strong character and prepares students for future challenges. Our goal is to provide equal learning opportunities for every child including economically weaker students. We focus on discipline, strong academic base and holistic development.
